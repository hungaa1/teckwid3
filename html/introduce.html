Introduction

We often throw away clothes when we no longer use them. We buy clothes at the mall, online stores or local stores, and when these clothes are no longer used, they throw them in the trash. This happens even with expensive clothes.

People who can buy expensive clothes usually won't wear clothes again many times. They buy newer items and usually only use each item 2-3 times. Number Old clothes will be thrown away. This situation is damaging to the planet because the number discarded clothes are increasing (although the quality is still very good), and more and more Many people give up their clothes.

Nowadays, recycling, reuse or sustainability are not just words, which many have taken action to protect the planet. Instead of tossing old clothes are still good, these clothes can be sold at stores thrift or used clothing brands.

Simply put, 2hand Universe is about shopping for second hand clothes.

This project promotes recycling and sustainability actions, and also provides affordable clothing for individuals who can't afford new clothes completely.

2hand Universe builds a friendly brand through the way they I collect used products and dispose of them cleanly tidy and bring the product to your hand. A friendly product provided by a friendly service is the guide to success our work.

2hand Universe will provide an easy way to shop for the costumes Used, new condition, convenient for customers to shop from anywhere, anytime 24/7.
